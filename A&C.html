Arts & Communication

Broadcasting & Production

This class teaches the fundamentals of media production for TV, radio, and digital platforms. Students learn camera work, sound editing, video production, and how to create engaging content. It also explores storytelling techniques and the production process for both live and recorded media.

Art & Performance

Students in this class explore various forms of creative expression, including visual arts, theater, music, and dance. The course focuses on developing artistic skills while understanding the cultural and emotional impact of performance and creation in different mediums.

Journalism

This course covers the basics of news reporting, writing, and editing. Students learn how to research, interview, and produce stories for print, online, and broadcast media. The course also focuses on journalistic ethics and the role of the media in society.

Digital Media

This course equips students with essential skills in multimedia production, including digital video editing, sound design, and storytelling techniques. Audio/Visual Production or Student Media: Broadcast

Mass Media Production

Focuses on the creation, editing, and distribution of media content for broad audiences. Students gain skills in storytelling, digital editing, and content creation for mass communication platforms.
