Arts And Communication

Broadcasting and Production

This course covers the essentials of radio and television production, focusing on storytelling, audio and video editing, and on-air presentation, with hands-on projects to develop practical skills.

Art & Performance

Students explore various forms of art and performance, including visual arts and theater, fostering creativity through workshops and a final exhibition to showcase their work.

Journalism

This course introduces the principles of journalism, emphasizing reporting, writing, and ethics, while equipping students with the skills to research stories and produce compelling content for various media.
