Public Service Network

Criminal Justice

This course explores the fundamentals of the criminal justice system, including law enforcement, the judiciary, and corrections.

Fire Science

Students learn about fire prevention, firefighting techniques, and emergency response strategies to prepare for a career in fire services.

Emergency Management

This course focuses on disaster preparedness, response strategies, and recovery planning to manage emergencies effectively.

Public Administration

Students gain insight into the operations of government agencies and the principles of public policy, management, and governance.

Social Work

This course introduces students to the field of social work, focusing on community service, advocacy, and support for individuals and families in need.

Health Services

Students explore healthcare systems, public health policies, and the role of health services in communities, preparing for careers in healthcare administration.
