Available Courses

Cybersecurity

Learn to protect systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. This is the art of cyber-war.

Computer Science and Software Development

Learn to design, create, and maintain software programs. The art of game making and game playing.

Engineering

Learn to design and build machines, structures, and processes, and improve systems. The art of our everyday world.

Informatics

Learn the study of information and computational systems, and how people interact with information and technology. The art of how you interact with your phones.

Unique Career Paths

Software Engineer

A professional who designs, develops, tests, and maintains software applications and computer systems.

Mechanical Engineer"

Helps designs, analyzes, manufactures, and maintains mechanical systems.

Data Scientist

A professional who uses scientific methods to extract insights from data to help organizations make decisions and solve problems.

Systems Architect

An IT professional who designs, builds, and maintains computer systems and networks.

Robotics Engineer

Helps design, build, and maintain robots and robotic systems for a variety of industries.

AI Developer

Writes code to implement AI algorithms and models, typically using programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++.
