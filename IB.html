Arts And Communication

Creativity, Action, Service

This course integrates creative projects, physical activities, and community service. Students will explore artistic expression, engage in sports, and participate in service projects, developing skills in self-expression, teamwork, and social responsibility.

Therory of Knowledge (TOK)

TOK examines the nature of knowledge and how we acquire it. Students will discuss different ways of knowing and explore various perspectives, encouraging critical thinking and questioning of assumptions.

Exctended Essay

In the Extended Essay, students conduct independent research on a topic of interest. They will formulate a research question, perform in-depth investigations, and write a 4,000-word essay to enhance their research and writing skills.
