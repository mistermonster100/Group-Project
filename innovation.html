Auto & Collision Repair

Build in-demand skills for repairing, repainting, and restoring damaged vehicles. Collision Repair training will show you how to make cars, trucks and SUVs look and run like new.

Automotive

Cover a whole range of topics related to self-propelled vehicles, including their design, engineering, mechanics, and computer technology.

Unique Career Paths

Product Manager

A professional who is responsible for the development, launch, and management of a product or service.

Innovation Consultant

Helps businesses and organizations develop new ideas and strategies to solve problems and achieve growth

Research & Development Specialist

Conducting research, analyzing data, and developing innovative solutions to enhance the company's products and services.

Technology Entrepreneur

Someone who starts a new company in the technology or biotechnology industry with the goal of creating a positive impact on a large scale.

An organization to help them understand more about their target market and ensure their products or services deal with the challenges of the target customers. They are becoming more and more prominent in organizations today.
