Medical Network

Certified Nursing Assistant

This course provides the foundational skills needed to assist nurses in patient care, including basic medical procedures, patient hygiene, and vital signs monitoring.

Medical Assisting

Students will learn administrative and clinical skills necessary for working in medical offices, including patient management, medical billing, and laboratory procedures.

Emergency Medical Technician

This course covers the skills needed to provide emergency care to patients in various situations, including CPR, first aid, and basic life support techniques.

Pre-Med

This course prepares students for a future in medical school, focusing on biology, chemistry, and health sciences, along with hands-on experience in clinical settings.

Biomedical Science

Students will explore the biological and physical sciences that underpin healthcare, including anatomy, physiology, and medical ethics.

Exercise Science

This course examines the scientific principles behind physical activity, focusing on fitness assessment, exercise prescription, and the role of exercise in health.
