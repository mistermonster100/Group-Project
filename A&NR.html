Available Courses

Veterinary Science

This course introduces students to animal health care, covering anatomy, common veterinary practices, and hands-on experience in labs and field trips.

Agriscience

Students explore the scientific foundations of agriculture, engaging in hands-on activities related to soil health, plant biology, and animal science.

Agricultural Systems

This course covers the components of agricultural systems, including crop production and livestock management, emphasizing their environmental and economic impacts.

Global Sustainability

Students examine sustainability principles globally, focusing on environmental, economic, and social issues, and explore solutions for climate change and resource management.

Unique Career Paths

Environmental Scientist

The knowledge of the natural sciences to protect the environment and human health.

Agricultural Manager

Manages the day-to-day operations of an agricultural establishment, such as a farm, ranch, greenhouse, or nursery.

Wildlife Biologist

The study animals and their habitats, and use their findings to develop conservation plans and inform the public.

Conservation Officer

Enforces the environmental and conservation laws and policies that are specific to their state.

Sustainable Agriculture Consultant

The essential service that helps farmers, agribusinesses, and organizations implement practices that are environmentally friendly, economically viable, and socially responsible

Renewable Energy Specialist

Assists in studies on emerging technologies, understand environmental impacts of energy production, transfer their understanding to practical business strategies, and communicate though a variety of means, including public presentations.
